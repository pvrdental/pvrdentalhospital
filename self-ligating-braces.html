Types of braces in Chennai

METAL BRACES in Chennai

Traditional braces use elastic ties to hold arch wires in place. Traditional braces produce better results for patients who want to straighten significantly crooked teeth, and young teenagers whose look with braces doesn’t affect their carrier. Unfortunately, elastic ties create friction which can slow treatment down. Elastics ties have also been shown to collect harmful bacteria.

Self ligating Braces @PVR Dental

The Damon System in Chennai utilizes a patented slide mechanism to connect arch wires to the bracket while allowing the wire to move freely. The Damon System @ PVR Dental reduces friction and improves oral hygiene by eliminating elastic ties, PVR Dental and orthodontic centre is the leading speciality centre for braces.

The Damon Braces Difference

Why to choose self ligating braces?

Damon braces used in PVR Dental & Orthodontic centre gives you a broader and healthier smile in less time with greater comfort than conventional braces

Natural broad arch smile
Smiles with a broad arch show more teeth and fill the corners of the mouth more fully. The result is a more natural and appealing smile. A broad arch also provides facial support, which gives the face a more youthful appearance.

Smile arc

An important aspect of a beautiful smile is the smile arc. A smile arc is created when the curve of the teeth follows the curve of the lower lip, creating a naturally beautiful and flowing smile.

Treating a gummy smile
A smile with gingival excess, also known as a ‘gummy’ smile, can distract from the natural beauty of the teeth and face. Our orthodontist at PVR Dental implant & Invisalign centre can now treat gummy smiles to incredible results

A beautiful smile is a good balance between gums and teeth.

HEALTHIER FOR YOU

The light, gentle forces used by the self ligating System promote efficient tooth movement and help improve oral health by preserving tooth roots. Lighter forces also help prevent undue gum recession and tooth loss.

The elastic ties of traditional braces have been proven to attract and collect plaque and bacteria. Because it does not require elastic ties, the Damon System helps improve oral hygiene and protect gum health.
