Dental implants for missing one or more teeth

Home   >   Dental implants for missing one or more teeth

Dental Implants are replacements for tooth roots and missing or damaged tooth. The procedure replaces damaged or missing tooth with Titanium screw-like posts and artificial ceramic teeth that look and functions like normal tooth.

In PVR Dental implant centre we provide world’s best dental implants with the greatest long term success for patients seeking cosmetic dental treatments and rehabilitation of lost teeth. We have given maximum number of successful treated cases in dental implant treatments using the latest implant techniques, highly trained staff ensures prompt delivery of aesthetic solutions ensuring strict quality control.

The dental implant used @ PVR Dental is made up of medical grade Titanium, it is bio-compatible to the tissues of the mouth. Therefore, they do not cause any side effects in the oral cavity.

TIME FRAME & SITTINGS

Placement of single dental implant placement usually takes about 30 – 60 minutes.

Dental implants treatment consists of two stages

Stage 1 – Implant placement procedure.
The implant placement is performed under local anaesthesia or conscious sedation, The Implant specialist reflects your gums and drills a hole into the bone and widens it to place the dental implant. Usually it takes 3- 6 months for the complete union of bone to implant to take place inconvenience.

Stage 2 – Prosthetic procedure.
Assessment of bone surrounding the implants following which new tooth or teeth can be fixed.
 

What is the success rate for dental implant surgery?
Overall, the success rate for dental implants is high—from 92–98%.

How do I care for my implants?
Home care for your implants consists of brushing and flossing. Regular dental visits to PVR Dental required for long-term health and success.

The implants could be done for one tooth to several teeth or the entire teeth set. Dental implants are better alternatives to conventional ‘bridges’

What are the advantages of dental implants?
Teeth made on dental implants look like natural teeth. They do not look artificial. Patients have reported a very high level of satisfaction with the appearance of teeth on dental implants. Placement of dental implants helps to reduce this loss of bone. Dental implants are more comfortable than removable dentures. They are as efficient as natural teeth for chewing food. They can be maintained the same way as normal teeth, with regular tooth brushing and flossing.
 

How do I know if dental implants are possible for me?
Following a detailed history, clinical examination and diagnostic imaging (X-rays and CT Scans), our team will discuss the possibilities of using dental implants to replace missing teeth in the mouth. Most patients are good candidates for receiving dental implants, followed by a 3-6 month healing period, after which the implant is placed.

What is the minimum age for dental implant treatment?
Dental implants should be placed only after growth of the patient has stopped, which is around 18 years. This ensures that the implant is in the right place to hold the future tooth after the completion of growth.

All types of implant treatment ranging from conventional implantology to more advanced techniques such as All-on-4 and Zygomatic implants are done at PVR Dental.
