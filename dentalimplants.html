What are dental Implants

Dental implants are solutions for replacing your missing or damaged tooth with Titanium screw-like posts and artificial ceramic teeth. Titanium used at PVR dental is medical-grade, bio-compatible to the mouth tissues, causing no side effects. PVR Dental is one of the best provider of dental implants in Chennai.

Benefits of dental implants

 The replaced teeth look and function naturally for chewing food and for regular brushing and flossing.

There is a high level of satisfaction with the appearance of teeth after dental implants.

It Helps reduces loss of bone and comfortable than removable dentures.

Procedure

We will examine the detailed history, clinical examination, and diagnostic imaging (X-Ray and CT Scans).

After examination, we will report on the possibilities of dental implants for replacing the tooth.

After Approval, The specialist will start the implant under local anesthesia.

The specialist then reveals your gum, drills a hole into the bone to widen it, and places the new teeth on the area.

The complete dental implant procedure takes 3-6 months of the healing period.

All on four implants

All four implant treatment uses only four implants, unlike traditional implants where 5-8 implants are needed in one arch.

All four implants do not require a bone grafting procedure as the implants are placed in strategic areas of the jaw with the available jaw bone, eliminating the cost and time.

All on 4 Implant treatments save your time and money as well as repeated surgeries and discomfort.

At PVR, Dental treatment is a cost-efficient, graft-less solution that provides patients with fixed full-arch prostheses on the day of surgery.

Full-arch rehabilitation with only four implants Two straight anterior implants and two implants tilted up to 45º in the posterior Immediate Function (fixed provisional bridge) For patients meeting criteria for immediate loading of implants graft-less procedure, Bone grafting is avoided by tilting the posterior implants, utilizing available bone. If you have twelve or more than twelve missing teeth and want to get them replaced, then all-on-4 dental implants are the best treatment choice for you.

Zygoma implants

The zygomatic implant is an evidence-based surgical and prosthetic solution for atrophic maxilla, an alternative to the grafting process.

Zygomatic implants are done in zygomatic bone when there is insufficient bone in the upper jaw.

There is no need for a bone grafting procedure. Branemark Zygoma system is graft-less where the implant is done directly in the cheekbones.

The zygomatic implant is done under general anesthesia and performed by an experienced oral and maxillofacial surgeon.
